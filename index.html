About Me

Kevin James is a self‑proclaimed “code whisperer” who’s been wrangling bugs and bending semicolons to his will since the age of dial‑up internet. Known for his unmatched ability to drink three cups of coffee before 9 AM, Kevin has built everything from suspiciously over‑engineered to‑do lists to websites that only work in Internet Explorer “because it’s funny.” When he’s not battling compiler errors or convincing CSS to behave, you’ll find him explaining to friends why “it works on my machine” is a valid excuse. Kevin’s mission in life is simple: write code so elegant that even future‑Kevin won’t complain about maintaining it.
